Player of the Month(POTM)

The award for the best player of the month goes to the player that we feel has made the most impact to the club for the month, in terms of their contribution to the team, goals, assists and resilience. For this month we feel no one is more deserving after that astounding bicycle kick which is a worthy contender for the goal of the season, than our very own number 49 Alejandro Garnacho.
